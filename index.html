THE APEX

Unprecedented luxury living. A meticulous fusion of nature, modern architecture, and precision engineering.

MAIN VOLUMES

Expansive 6-meter double-height ceilings enveloped in low-E thermal-glazed floor-to-ceiling windows, maximizing natural light while maintaining climate efficiency.

VERTICAL ASCENT

Striking vertical facades forged from fair-faced exposed concrete and black basalt stone, asserting architectural power and timeless durability.

INFINITY OASIS

A spectacular 18.5m x 5m heated infinity pool featuring a submerged lounging ledge, clad in dark Indonesian Sukabumi stone for a deep ocean hue.

MINIMALIST GROUNDS

Exquisitely curated landscaping utilizing drought-resistant native flora, complemented by subtle low-voltage pathway ambient lighting.

FLOATING CONSOLES

Revolutionary 4-meter cantilevered balconies featuring brushed steel framing and invisible rimless glass balustrades for uninterrupted panoramic views.

THE APEX VISION

Born from an uncompromising desire to blend raw natural beauty with extreme modern minimalism. THE APEX is not just a residence, it is a statement of living. Every detail, from the structural integrity of the basalt foundations to the millimeter-perfect alignments of the glass expanses, has been obsessively calculated to provide a serene sanctuary decoupled from the chaotic world.

REFINED ARCHITECTURE

Designed by acclaimed international architects, emphasizing clean geometric lines, seamless spatial flow, and passive solar design principles. The intersecting volumes generate shaded outdoor patios during the summer, while the deep recessed floor-to-ceiling glass maximizes thermal retention in the winter.

PRIME LOCATION

Situated in an exclusive gated plateau 500 meters above sea level. This unique geographical vantage point offers 180-degree unobstructed cinematic ocean and valley aesthetics. Extreme privacy meets absolute accessibility.

MASTER PLANS

Over 850 sqm of meticulously planned interior space spanning three levels features rigorous zoning: public entertaining quarters seamlessly transitioning to the pool deck, while private family sanctuaries reside elevated and secluded.
